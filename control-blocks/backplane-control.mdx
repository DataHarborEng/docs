# Backplane Control

**Infrastructure-level access controls.**

Backplane Control provides group rate limiting and IP restrictions for your Virtual APIs, giving you fine-grained control over request volume and network-level access.

## Group rate limiting

Control request volume per consumer group across your Virtual APIs.

## IP restrictions

Restrict access to specific IP ranges to ensure only authorized networks can reach your data.
