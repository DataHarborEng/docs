# Deliver Data

Delivery is the last part of the DataHarbor story: once the payload is normalized, matched, and governed, DataHarbor returns it in the shape and format your consumer needs.

This section is organized around the delivery half of the pipeline:

1. How requests flow through REST and MCP.
2. How upstream payloads normalize before controls run.
3. How governed responses format as JSON, Markdown, CSV, or YAML.
